Project Sandstorms

A partnership with Prince Mohammad Bin Fahd University

About Project Sandstorms

In 2020, Flax & Teal began a partnership with Prince Mohammad Bin Fahd University in Saudi Arabia, named Project Sandstorms. It involves collaborating with Saudi Arabian educators to focus on the translation functionality of Our Raging Planet, allowing us to provide the site in Arabic, and allowing students to replicate sandstorm simulations to see the impact these disasters have within Riyadh and Dhahran.
